A Message from the Business Owner:

I started SVCrafted in January 2021 as a small business that offers quality customized clothing and gift items.
Throughout my life, I've always loved crafting and making gifts for my friends and family, so starting this business seemed like a natural way to merge these two passions.
I am fully responsible for finance management, advertisement, product design, communication with customers and organizations, production, and shipment.
As a student, I understand the importance of having products with a varying price range. I value making my products accessible to everyone, regardless of their budget.
I also try to support meaningful causes and organizations such as the Canadian Mental Health Association and Black Lives Matter movement.
My sales are currently completed via Etsy, through which I've made over 50 sales since launching my business.
In the same three months, I have completed bulk orders of up to 60 items/order with 3 companies, 3 student organizations, and 1 not-for-profit organization.

Sincerely,
Sophie Ciardullo
